# Template Catalog

## ranked-list-remotion

- Best for: rankings, plugin collections, tool lists, checklists, feature collections.
- Native format: `1080x1920`, 30 fps, about 12 seconds.
- Structure: title reveal, two-column card grid, staggered entrances, sequential focus, light sweep, complete-list hold.
- Strength: high information density with a clear final overview.
- Adapt when: names are long, descriptions need more than two lines, or the list has fewer/more than ten items.
- Use for vertical TOP lists, plugin lists, creator tool recommendations, and social short-video covers that need every item visible.

## orbit-opener-remotion

- Best for: many tools, logos, integrations, ecosystems, categories, or an energetic opening.
- Native format: `1920x1080`, 30 fps, about 15 seconds.
- Structure: scattered icon arrival, dual counter-rotating orbits, depth and trail effects, orbit shift, title entrance, overview cards, optional signature close.
- Strength: the orbit creates immediate visual impact, then resolves into useful information.
- Remove the sample signature unless personal branding is requested.
- Use when the first scene needs spectacle: plugin ecosystems, AI tool collections, brand partner walls, industry maps, or feature constellations.

## editorial-deck-hyperframes

- Best for: document-to-video, feature explainers, offline sharing, screenshot tutorials, and editorial presentations.
- Native format: `1920x1080`, scene durations usually 5-7 seconds.
- Structure: full-screen light paper, concise chapter scenes, source screenshots paired with claims, restrained hand-drawn lines and soft color accents.
- Strength: preserves detail and evidence without looking like a corporate dashboard.
- The bundled starter is generic: no QR codes, contact page, personal signature, or default music.
