## Style Prompt

Offline-share editorial slide video for a Codex feature update. Use a clean white presentation-card surface on a cool gray-blue stage, thin hand-drawn black/brown lines, soft translucent cyan/pink/yellow blobs, restrained serif display titles, and compact Chinese body copy. The feel should match premium PPT template references: airy, deliberate, polished, not corporate SaaS marketing.

## Colors

- Stage gray: `#a8adb8`
- Paper white: `#fbfbf8`
- Ink black: `#151515`
- Quiet gray: `#777d86`
- Line brown: `#786858`
- Aqua glow: `#70d4e8`
- Pink glow: `#f0b5d8`
- Lemon glow: `#f5d85b`
- Signal blue: `#1f5fba`

## Typography

- Display: `"Times New Roman", "Songti SC", "STSong", serif`
- Hand accent: `"Kaiti SC", "STKaiti", "KaiTi", cursive`
- UI/body: `-apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`

## Motion

- Slide cards breathe in with gentle y/scale movement.
- Text reveals in small staggered groups.
- Images should drift or scale very slightly, like a camera pass across a physical deck.
- Decorative curves draw on with stroke-dashoffset.
- Blobs float only subtly; never dominate the content.

## What Not To Do

- Do not use a dark tech dashboard look.
- Do not fill the frame with dense article paragraphs.
- Do not use purple-blue gradients as the main theme.
- Do not separate document images from the matching feature explanation.
- Keep creator branding optional and subtle; use a small corner label only when requested.
